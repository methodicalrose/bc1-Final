Quinn Callahan

Tattoo Artist

"I've been part of the tattoo culture for about five years, but I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. Back then I would draw something over and over, and try to get it to look just like the piece of art before it. Conformity to the norm. Since then I have shifted away and started doing my own designs, and now I can truly appreciate all of my art for the uniqueness that it brings to the world. That's why I do custom tattoos; to help people stand out from society, and show their unique story with pride. Choose to be your truest you."
